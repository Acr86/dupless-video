Dupless Video — BETA
====================

- This is a BETA. Take it easy.
- It finds DUPLICATE videos by CONTENT (not by filename).
- IMPORTANT: review before deleting. Deletions go to the Recycle Bin
  (recoverable) — do NOT empty it until you have confirmed.
- If it won't open or scan, run the diagnostic:
      "Dupless Video.exe" --selftest
  and share the file:  %LOCALAPPDATA%\Dupless Video\selftest.log
